Co Curricular Activites

Scouting inculcates sound values and life skills that will develop a person holistically. In Scouting, there is great emphasis to duty and service. We encourage our young Cub Scouts to do a good turn each day and through service, keeping in mind the Scout Promise and Law.

The KCS Chinese Orchestra aims to nurture the love of music in its members. Through regular combine and sectional practice sessions, members are given the opportunity to work and learn with each other to gain important life skills such as perseverance, responsibility, and teamwork.

The Environmental Science Club aims to promote environmental awareness and care for the environment both the school environment and our neighbourhood environment among our students.

As part of the Singapore Red Cross Youth, we seek to promote and encourage participation of our members in the work of Red Cross and to instil in them a sense of social responsibility towards the less fortunate.
